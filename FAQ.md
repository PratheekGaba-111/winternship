## 1. Internship Dates & Duration

- **Mode:** Online  
- **Start Date:** 22 December  
- **Duration:** 4 Weeks  
- **Working Days:** Monday to Saturday  
- **Timings:** 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

## 2. Mode & Attendance

- The internship is conducted **strictly online**
- **Attendance is mandatory** for all sessions
- A maximum of **2 warnings** may be issued for non-attendance
- Continued absence can lead to **termination**
- Interns must join sessions using the **same name and email ID** used during NPTEL registration
- Attendance issues related to NPTEL sheets must be raised **directly with NPTEL**

---

## 3. Mentorship & Interaction

- Course instructors **may interact** depending on the nature of the project
- Regular guidance is provided by:
  - Mentors
  - Coordinators

## 4. Support & Escalation Process

### Level 1: Support Ticket
Email:
    internship-support@vicharanashala.zohodesk.in

- Response time: Within **2 hours** (9:00 AM – 9:00 PM)
- Queries after 9:00 PM are addressed the next day

---

### Level 2: Follow-up (After 24 Hours)
- Raise a new ticket  
- Subject format:
    "Attention Please – Follow-up on Ticket #[Ticket Number]"

- Issue is escalated to the **Mentors Team**
- Resolution may happen via **voice call**

---

### Level 3: Final Escalation (After 48 Hours)
Email the Internship Sponsor and **CC**:
- Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar  
- Department Faculty Reference  
- Department HOD  
- DLED Team: `dled@iitrpr.ac.in`

Include:
- Full Name
- Department
- Ticket reference numbers
- Brief issue description

> Following this escalation process is **mandatory** for all interns.

## 9. ViBe Platform – Access & Issues

### Login Steps
1. Open the invite email from *vicharanashala*
2. Click **Accept Invite**
3. Select **Continue as Student**
4. Login using the registered Google email
5. Access the dashboard

---

### Common Issues & Resolutions

**No Course Enrolled**
- Verify the registered email
- Check **Profile → Invites**
- Contact support if the issue persists

**Video Not Playing**
- Watch videos sequentially
- Allow camera and microphone permissions
- Perform a hard refresh (`Ctrl + Shift + R`)

**Access Restricted**
- Ensure no steps were skipped
- Refresh the page and resume from the dashboard

**Quiz Marked Incorrect**
- Report the issue with video and case details
- Backend correction may take some time

**Progress Not Showing 100%**
- Refresh the browser
- Allow time for progress sync
- Organizer-maintained records are final
